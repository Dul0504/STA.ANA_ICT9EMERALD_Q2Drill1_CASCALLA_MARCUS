Types of Watch Brands

The Best Guide

Tissot

A Swiss brand known for offering high-quality, affordable luxury watches with a strong heritage in precision and sports timing.

Cartier

A prestigious French luxury brand famous for elegant, iconic watch designs such as the Tank and Santos, combining jewelry craftsmanship with watchmaking.

Bovet

A high-end Swiss watchmaker specializing in artistic, handcrafted timepieces known for intricate decorations and exceptional mechanical movements.

IWC

A Swiss luxury manufacturer known for engineering-focused watches like the Pilot’s and Portugieser collections, blending precision with rugged elegance.

TAG Heuer

A sporty Swiss brand recognized for chronographs and racing-inspired designs, offering durable performance watches with modern innovation.

Longines

A historic Swiss brand admired for classic, elegant timepieces and exceptional accuracy, with over 190 years of watchmaking tradition.
